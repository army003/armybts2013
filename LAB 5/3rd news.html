On November 27 and 28, and December 1 and 2, #BTS will host "BTS PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE" OFFLINE at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles.




On November 27 and 28, and December 1 and 2, #BTS will host "BTS PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE" OFFLINE at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles. THIS IS THE FIRST TIME BTS WILL GET FACE TO FACE WITH ARMY AFTER WORLD TOUR LOVE YOURSELF: SPEAK YOURSELF THE FINAL in 2019! Message from BigHit: Hello. This is BigHit Music. BTS PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE in person (offline) concert will take place in Los Angeles, USA. Having a face-to-face concert in the midst of COVID-19 is not easy, but after looking for opportunities to do so, we may be able to host a concert in the United States, taking into account national and regional health regulations and circumstances. At this concert, BTS and ARMY will meet face to face for the first time since BTS WORLD TOUR LOVE YOURSELF: SPEAK YOURSELF THE FINAL in 2019. This is an especially rewarding event that will take place in difficult circumstances, so we will do our best to bring you a completely new, phenomenal concert, while keeping the health of artists and fans our top priority. We deeply regret that we cannot hold more concerts in more venues. We will do our best to organize additional concerts not only for Korean fans, but also for fans from all over the world who have been patiently waiting for a long time. We look forward to your continued interest in BTS PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE - LA.
